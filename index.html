Stop restocking alone. Order together.

KSN links Embu electronics shops into a single sourcing hub — track your stock, submit your weekly restock list, and get it combined with every other shop's order into one bulk buy from Nairobi.

How it works

Three steps, once a week.

01

Track your stock

Log products, prices, and minimum stock levels. KSN flags what's running low automatically.

02

Build your restock list

Add low-stock items to your restock cart through the week, then submit it once ready.

03

We combine and buy

Every shop's order is merged into one bulk purchase from Nairobi — better prices, one delivery run.
